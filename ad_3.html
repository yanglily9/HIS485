This male marriage advertisement was published on May 24, 1920.[7] He introduces himself and claimed that he has million (家资百万) and lives in Japan. He studied at the Empire University for Economics but now he dropped out. He claims to have a kind personality, and he is a patriot. He will graduate next year and attend to graduate school in United States for research. He says he will move back to Shanghai after his studying. He emphasizes that he has a house that is so big that he needs someone to handle household chores and stuff. Wants an ideal wife. If she is interested and fits the below requirements, she can send a photo and mail it to Japan. 1) She needs to have some education and understands english. 2) She needs to have a kind personality and patriot and modern thinking. 3) She needs to be healthy and outgoing and if too skinny or too fat will not passed (过肥过瘦均不合格). 4) She needs to have a nice face and “pure”(相貌须娇艳清白者) 5) She needs to be above seventeen and below twenty. 6) She needs to tell the truth. If she fits the above requirements she will have the opportunity to study abroad in Japan. She does not need to be wealthy or poor because husband's family will support everything. They will marry immediately. If her family is poor they will pay the girl's family.

Key point: This advertisement demonstrated the typical requirements for a potential female partner. Similar to other male advertisement that was collected, requirements for the wife was that her age had to be lower than the husband. She has to be beautiful and have a fit body.
